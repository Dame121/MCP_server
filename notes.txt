COMPUTERS NOTE: 
Modern computers have evolved tremendously from their early origins. They now range from powerful desktop workstations to portable laptops, tablets, and smartphones. Key components include processors (CPU), memory (RAM), storage (SSD/HDD), graphics processors, and various input/output devices. Most run operating systems like Windows, macOS, Linux, Android, or iOS. Computers have become essential tools for work, education, entertainment, and communication throughout society.
